Free and Easy Word Count Tool

This online word counter helps writers, students, bloggers, marketers, and professionals instantly check text length. It shows words, characters, characters without spaces, sentences, paragraphs, reading time, speaking time, and more in real time.

The improved layout is designed to feel cleaner and more modern on both desktop and mobile devices, while still keeping the tool fast and simple to use.

What Can This Tool Count?

  • Total words
  • Characters with spaces
  • Characters without spaces
  • Sentences and paragraphs
  • Reading, speaking, and handwriting time
  • Average word and sentence length
  • Unique word ratio

Notes and Saved Text

You can save your text as notes directly in your browser. This is useful for drafts, captions, assignments, blog ideas, and repeated writing work that you want to access later without losing the counts.

Who Can Use This Tool?

This tool is useful for students, writers, bloggers, journalists, copywriters, SEO specialists, social media managers, and anyone who needs accurate text measurement.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does this tool store my text online?

No. Your text and notes are stored in your browser unless you manually copy or export them.

Is this tool mobile-friendly?

Yes. The layout is optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile screens.

Can I save my notes?

Yes. You can save the current text as a named note and reopen it later from the Notes section.
