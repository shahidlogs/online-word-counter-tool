About Online Word Counter Tool

Online Word Counter Tool is a simple, fast, and free tool that helps users count words, characters, sentences, paragraphs, and reading time instantly.

Why This Tool Exists

Writers, students, bloggers, and professionals often need to know the exact length of their text. This tool was created to provide a clean and easy way to check text statistics without installing software.

What This Tool Can Do

  • Count words
  • Count characters with spaces
  • Count characters without spaces
  • Count sentences
  • Count paragraphs
  • Estimate reading time

Fast and Private

All text is processed directly in your browser. We do not store the text you enter for normal tool usage.

Free to Use

This website is free to use and will remain free. In the future, ads may be used to support the cost of hosting and development.
