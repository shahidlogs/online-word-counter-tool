Privacy Policy

This page explains how Online Word Counter Tool works, what limited information may be used, and how browser-based features such as saved notes and theme preferences operate.

Your Privacy Matters

Online Word Counter Tool values your privacy. This page explains what information may be involved when you use this website and how the tool works in a normal browser session.

Information We Collect

This website does not require account registration and does not directly ask for personal details such as your name, phone number, or email address for normal use of the tool.

Text You Enter

Text entered into the word counter tool is processed inside your browser to calculate word count, character count, sentence count, paragraph count, reading time, speaking time, and related statistics. For normal tool usage, your text is not uploaded or stored by the website server.

Local Browser Storage

Some features, such as saved notes, auto-save, and theme preference, may use your browser’s local storage. This means the information stays on your own device and browser unless you delete it or clear your browser data.

Cookies and Analytics

This website may use analytics tools or cookies to understand traffic, improve performance, and enhance the user experience. These tools may collect general usage data such as pages viewed, device type, browser type, and approximate location information.

Advertising

This website may display ads from third-party advertising partners such as Google AdSense. These services may use cookies to provide relevant ads, limit repeated ads, and measure ad performance.

Third-Party Links

This website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, security, or content of third-party sites.

Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the website, legal requirements, analytics tools, or advertising policies. Updated versions will be posted on this page.

Children’s Privacy

This website is a general utility tool and is not specifically directed toward children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through normal use of the tool.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please visit the contact page of this website.
